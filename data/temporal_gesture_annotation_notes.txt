Just Alvin 24 Agustus 2014 - Golden Voices (https://video.medcom.id/just-alvin/280)


This is about famous Indonesian singers who have distinctive voices/vocals. The host tries to elicit when they realise that their voices are unique as singers. This question is essential in inviting language about time, and most importantly the accompanying gesture when they talk about time.
